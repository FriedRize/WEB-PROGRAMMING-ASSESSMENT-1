One Piece Film: Red

*One Piece Film: Red* is the fifteenth feature film of the popular *One Piece* anime series. Released in 2022 and it focuses on Uta the world-renowned singer and the daughter of Red-Haired Shanks. During her concert secrets of her past and the connection to Luffy and the Straw Hat Pirates unravel leading to an epic confrontation that shakes the world. The film blends music and action and emotion in an adventure that explores themes of freedom and friendship and truth.

Key Information

  • Genre: Action, Adventure, Fantasy, Musical
  • Director: Goro Taniguchi
  • Writer: Tsutomu Kuroiwa
  • Original Creator: Eiichiro Oda
  • Release Date: August 6, 2022 (Japan)
  • Studio: Toei Animation
  • Runtime: 115 minutes

Main Characters

  • Monkey D. Luffy: Captain of the Straw Hat Pirates, determined to become the King of the Pirates.
  • Uta: A famous singer and Shanks' daughter, whose voice holds incredible power.
  • Red-Haired Shanks: One of the Four Emperors, Shanks is a legendary pirate and Uta's father.
  • Nami, Zoro, Usopp, Sanji, Chopper, Robin, Franky, Brook, Jinbe: The loyal members of the Straw Hat crew, each bringing unique skills to the adventure.
