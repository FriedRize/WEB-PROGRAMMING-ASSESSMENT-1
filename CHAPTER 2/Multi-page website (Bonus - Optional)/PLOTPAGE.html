Plot

*One Piece Film: Red* focuses on Uta the world's most beloved singer and daughter of Red-Haired Shanks. During her long-awaited concert her shocking secret is revealed. As Luffy and his crew reunite with Uta they become entangled in an epic event that threatens the world itself. The film explores themes of freedom and truth and the bonds between family and friends.
