Main Characters

  • Monkey D. Luffy: Captain of the Straw Hat Pirates, with dreams of becoming King of the Pirates.
  • Uta: A famous singer with a powerful voice and daughter of Shanks.
  • Red-Haired Shanks: Legendary pirate, one of the Four Emperors, and Uta's father.
  • The Straw Hat Crew: Nami, Zoro, Usopp, Sanji, Chopper, Robin, Franky, Brook, and Jinbe each bring their unique talents to the crew.
